Awards & Honors

Bharat Ratna Dr. C. Subramaniam Award

Dr. Dhirendra Khare was honoured with the Bharat Ratna Dr. C. Subramaniam Award for Outstanding Teacher in Agriculture and Allied Sciences by the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) for the biennium 2007–2008. This prestigious recognition reflects his exceptional contributions to agricultural education and his long-standing commitment to academic excellence.

About the Award

  • Awarding Body: Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR)
  • Category: Outstanding Teacher in Agriculture and Allied Sciences
  • Biennium: 2007–2008
  • Recipient: Dr. Dhirendra Khare, Professor of Plant Breeding & Genetics, JNKVV, Jabalpur

Significance

This award is considered one of ICAR’s most prestigious honours, conferred upon educators who demonstrate outstanding excellence in agricultural teaching, innovation in pedagogy, and meaningful contributions to student mentorship and institutional development. It recognises individuals who have played a pivotal role in shaping the future of agricultural education in the country.

Contributions Leading to the Award

Dr. Khare’s work in curriculum development, research-led teaching, mentorship of postgraduate and doctoral scholars, and his initiatives to strengthen practical, field-oriented learning significantly contributed to this recognition. His efforts consistently bridged classroom learning with real-world agricultural challenges, helping to create a strong foundation for future scientists and professionals in the agricultural sector.
