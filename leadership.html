Leadership & Institutional Roles

Throughout my academic career, I have been entrusted with significant leadership responsibilities that allowed me to contribute meaningfully to the advancement of agricultural education, institutional governance, and research development. My work has consistently focused on strengthening academic structures, promoting innovation, and aligning educational initiatives with national agricultural priorities.

At Jawaharlal Nehru Krishi Vishwa Vidyalaya (JNKVV), Jabalpur, I had the privilege of serving in several senior roles that played a key part in shaping institutional policies and academic direction.

Key leadership positions held:

  • Dean, Faculty of Agriculture, JNKVV, Jabalpur
  • Director of Research, JNKVV, Jabalpur
  • Director of Instruction, JNKVV, Jabalpur
  • Dean, College of Agriculture, JNKVV, Jabalpur

Through these roles, I actively contributed to curriculum modernization, research capacity building, policy formulation, and the promotion of sustainable agricultural practices. My leadership initiatives focused on creating a strong academic foundation while encouraging innovation, collaboration, and excellence in teaching and research.
