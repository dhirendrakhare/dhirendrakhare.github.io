About

Dr. Dhirendra Khare is a distinguished academician and scientist in Plant Breeding and Seed Technology. With over three decades of teaching and research experience, he has significantly contributed to agricultural education, crop improvement, and national seed systems in India.

Career & Journey

He began as Assistant Professor in 1985, later serving as Senior Scientist, Principal Scientist, and Dean. He also served as Director of Instruction and Director of Research at JNKVV, leading academic and research initiatives.
