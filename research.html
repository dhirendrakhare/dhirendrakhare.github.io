My Contributions to Research

My research journey has been shaped by a deep commitment to advancing agricultural science and delivering field-driven solutions that strengthen farming systems and agricultural sustainability.

I had the privilege of establishing and leading the Seed Technology Centre and Referral Seed Testing Laboratories under ICAR-sponsored initiatives, creating robust research frameworks and modern scientific infrastructure.

Capacity building has been central to my professional vision. I designed and delivered specialised training programmes for professionals engaged in seed production, certification, and agricultural trade across India.

Mentorship has always been a defining element of my academic life. Guiding undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral scholars has been both a responsibility and a passion, inspiring future leaders in agricultural science.
